Who i'm...

I am Gaia Sitri, a third year Digital Humanities student at the University of Pisa. I created this site as a project for the Web Design and Programming exam and it aims to offer a rough guide to the city of New York trying to highlight what are the places you can't miss if you visit this wonderful city!

I dedicated my university project to this city because when i visited it, years ago, it remained in my heart and it would be a dream for me to be able to go back there soon for a vacation, and maybe one day move there.

References

I have consulted several sites including:newyorkfacile regarding museums, unamicainviaggio for restaurants, and Wikipedia for attractions. The images were taken from Pixabay, and from Pinterest. The Javascript code in the quiz is taken from the book "Programmazione in Javascript" by Vincenzo Ambriola. Documents validated with the W3C validator.
